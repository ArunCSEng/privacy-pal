About BitDark

BitDark is a privacy-first safe link analyzer that helps you quickly judge whether a link looks risky before you open it.

Most scam links arrive through WhatsApp, SMS, random emails or social media DMs. They often pretend to be delivery updates, refunds, prize claims, or urgent account warnings.

BitDark does not visit or load the website. Instead, it analyzes the structure of the URL itself: strange subdomains, suspicious top-level domains, brand lookalikes, phishing words, excessive encoding, and more. All of this happens entirely in your browser, using JavaScript only.

Because nothing is sent to a server, there is no central logging of URLs, no tracking of what you scan, and no account required. It is a simple tool to give you a quick safety hint before you decide what to do next.

BitDark does not guarantee that a green score means a website is safe. It simply checks for common red flags used in scams and phishing campaigns. You should always stay cautious with unknown senders, offers that look too good to be true, or messages that create urgency or fear.
